I stepped out into the real world recently to practice design. Outside college projects where everybody is there to encourage you to think differently, wildly. Outside internships with very little expectations on you. I began practicing design on real projects, with real stakes and people who are just as committed to bringing something to reality. 

In the months that I have spent working, there has been a lot of learnings, mostly to do with navigating through a company in order to get your ideas seen and built.
